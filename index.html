Calabar, Nigeria

Nigeria, known as the "Giant of Africa," is the most populous country on the continent, with over 200 million people. It is a diverse nation with more than 250 ethnic groups and a rich cultural heritage. From the bustling city of Lagos to the serene landscapes of the Niger Delta, Nigeria is a land of contrasts and opportunities.

Nigerians are known for their resilience, creativity, and vibrant spirit. The country has a thriving arts scene, with Nollywood being one of the largest film industries in the world. Nigerian music, particularly Afrobeat, has gained international acclaim. The people are also passionate about sports, especially football (soccer), and the national team, the Super Eagles, has a large following.

Nigeria's economy is one of the largest in Africa, driven by oil and gas production, agriculture, and a growing tech industry. The country is home to many startups and innovation hubs, making it a hotspot for tech enthusiasts and entrepreneurs.
